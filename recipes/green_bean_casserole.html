Green Bean Casserole

Grandmas Best Green Bean Casserole

Grandmas Best Green Bean Casserole is needed for any special occasion IMHO. Weather it be for a large family gathering or just for the heck of it, this recipe is a real crowd pleaser. Combined with our Chicken and Dressing Casserole this fan favorite will make a feast for your family at dinnertime!

Ingredients

  • 2 cans (14.5oz cans) french-style green beans
  • 1 can cream of mushroom soup
  • 1/2 coup of milk
  • 1 tsp. soy sauce
  • 1 dash black pepper
  • 1 1/3 cups French's crispy fried onions
  • 1 can mushrooms
*Preheat oven to 350 degrees*
  1. Mix soup, milk, soy sauce, and pepper in 1 1/2 quart baking dish. Stir in beans, mushrooms, and 2/3 cup fried onion.
  2. Bake 30 minutes
  3. Top with remaining 2/3 cup fried onions. Bake an additional 5 minutes or until onions are golden brown.
